How to Gain Fluency in Language Without Focusing on Grammar Study

Here are 14 ways on how you can achieve fluency in just 6 months. By doing the following in a regular basis in your daily routine eventually you will get the fluency that you've been aiming for.

1. Read any reading materials that is written or translated in the language you are learning.

2. Listen to DJs talk and teachers' lecture when they are talking in the language you are learning.

3. Watch movies, TV shows, talk shows, news, even TV commercials that are using the language you are learning.

4. Correspond to chat mates, text mates in the language you are learning.

5. Listen to different genres of music especially rock and blues, memorize and study the lyrics.

6. Do some Video Karaoke or sing along activities and just sing only songs in the language you are learning.

7. Browse the web, post blogs and study different blogs about the language you are learning.

8. Search for videos about discussions or lessons on YouTube or any internet site.

9. Whisper to yourself or talk to yourself using the language you are learning when no one is around.

10. Talk to your friends, teachers, bosses, workmates using the second language.

11. Meet and talk to native speakers to acquire accent.

12. Do self study or enroll yourself in any proficiency course or program to know more about grammar rules and technical aspects.

13. Widen your vocabulary and make it a point to study new words and phrases.

14. Practice and use the language you are learning more often.
